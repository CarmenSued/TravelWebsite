Transport Information

To get to Tarifa you have various options, but here we will recommend the most accesible transportation method. From the two airports nearby which are in Gibraltar and Malaga, we highly recomend to flight to Malaga beacause has more flights availables than Gibraltar. Hire a taxi or take a bus to get to the train station is available 24/7 at the airport, but if you prefer to drive until Tarifa and see the landscape of Andalucia, then rent a car is the best option, Tarifa is just one hour and a half from Malaga airport. The good thing about to rent a car is the benefit of travel around the town and visit other places, included the city of Cadiz.
